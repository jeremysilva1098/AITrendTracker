Title: With landmark AI Act, the EU takes the lead in regulating artificial intelligence

Source: Android Central

Summary:

  • The European Union has reached a preliminary agreement for the AI Act, which is the first successful attempt at AI regulation.
  • The AI Act aims to curb potential risks of advanced automation and misinformation, focusing on limiting malicious applications of AI such as predictive policing and manipulating human behavior.
  • Big tech companies will be targeted under the "high-risk systems" provisions, requiring compliance with transparency requirements and potential fines of up to 7% of revenue for non-compliance.
  • The AI Act still needs formal adoption, but its enactment is highly likely, although the provisions may not be enforceable for another year or two.

Title: My Surprisingly Unbiased Week With Elon Musk's 'Politically Biased' Chatbot

Summary:

Title: NotebookLM publicly launches in the US using Google's new Gemini Pro AI model

Source: Android Central

Summary:

  • Google's NotebookLM, an AI-equipped notebook assistant, is now publicly available to users in the US.
  • The assistant uses Google's new Gemini Pro AI model and is designed to help users work smarter.
  • NotebookLM differs from other AI chatbots by only using sources or files uploaded by the user to provide answers and information.
  • Google warns that NotebookLM may still provide inaccurate responses, but it offers a more accurate way to get help from AI.
  • The public rollout of NotebookLM includes 16 new features based on feedback from early access testers, including the ability to save interactions as notes and suggested actions.
