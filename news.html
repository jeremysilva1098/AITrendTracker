Title: Should AI play an ever-growing role in tackling crime?

Source: BBC News

Summary:

  • Artificial intelligence (AI) is increasingly being used by police forces around the world to improve efficiency in handling emergency calls.
  • AI-powered software can transcribe calls, retrieve relevant information from police databases, and provide a triaging score to determine the urgency of the situation.
  • However, there are concerns about the use of AI in law enforcement, including the accuracy and bias of facial recognition software and the potential for biased historical data in predictive policing algorithms.
  • Independent testing has shown that facial recognition software has improved accuracy but still has a higher likelihood of false positive identification for black faces compared to white or Asian faces.
  • Critics argue that more analysis and evaluation are needed to ensure the validity and fairness of AI tools used in policing.
