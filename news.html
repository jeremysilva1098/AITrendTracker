Staying One Step Ahead of Hackers When It Comes to AI

Source: Wired

Summary:

  • Generative AI has the potential to facilitate new forms of cybercrime, such as personalized phishing emails that are convincing and effective.
  • Deepfaking made possible by AI can make biometric hacking easier and less costly, as it can be used to impersonate voices or other biometric authentication methods.
  • Hackers may target chatbots themselves by injecting malware into the objects generated by chatbots.
  • The "unexplainability" of generative AI algorithms can lead to chatbots being used for unethical purposes, such as providing advice to commit criminal acts or promoting harmful ideologies.
  • While AI can be used for offense, it can also be used for defense, with developers leveraging AI to enhance the security of digital systems.

Get Ready for the Great AI Disappointment

Source: Wired

Summary:

  • The article predicts that there will be a "Great AI Disappointment" in 2024, as the hype around generative AI and large language models will be overshadowed by their underwhelming performance and dangerous results.
  • The technology will face setbacks as evidence emerges that it provides false information and is prone to hallucination, where the AI makes things up and gets it wrong.
  • The article suggests that hopes for quick fixes to these problems will prove optimistic, as the architecture of these models makes it difficult to anchor predictions to known truths.
  • Anticipation for exponential improvements in productivity and progress towards artificial general intelligence (AGI) will also be disappointed, and blame for failures will shift to faulty implementation by businesses.
  • The biggest use of generative AI will be in social media and online search, leading to increased manipulation, misinformation, and mental health problems associated with excessive screen time.

Summary:

  • Lenovo has unveiled new versions of its Yoga Pro 9i and Yoga 9i 2-in-1 laptops, which now come with AI chips and a generative art suite.
