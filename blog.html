Title: Anthropic says its latest AI bot can beat Gemini and ChatGPT

Source: The Verge

Summary:

  • Anthropic has announced the next generation of its AI bot, Claude 3, which is multimodal and claims to be more capable than ChatGPT and Google Gemini.
  • Claude 3 models can answer more questions, understand longer instructions, and provide more accurate results.
  • The new models have improved contextual understanding and are less likely to refuse to answer prompts.
  • Claude 3 Opus, the largest model, outperformed OpenAI's GPT-4 in benchmarking tests and showed better reasoning abilities.
  • The models are trained on a mix of internal and third-party datasets and will be available on AWS's Bedrock and Google's Vertex AI.

Title: Google reshapes Fitbit in its image as users allege “planned obsolescence”

Source: Ars Technica

Summary:

  • Google has made significant changes to Fitbit since its acquisition, including introducing generative AI features.
  • Fitbit is working with Google Research and health experts to develop a large language model (LLM) for personalized coaching features.
  • Some long-time Fitbit users are expressing frustration and regretting their purchases due to ongoing issues, such as rapid battery drain on the Charge 5.
  • Users have accused Fitbit of planned obsolescence and are considering alternative fitness trackers.
  • Fitbit has not provided sufficient explanations or solutions to address these issues, leading to dissatisfaction among users.

Title: Anthropic claim new Claude 3 AI chatbot outperforms ChatGPT and Gemini

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Anthropic has launched Claude 3, a new chatbot that claims to have reached near-human levels of performance.
  • Claude 3 outperforms ChatGPT and Google Gemini in several industry benchmarks.
  • The three models of Claude 3 (Haiku, Sonnet, and Opus) offer different levels of power and functionality.
  • Opus, the largest-capacity model, displays higher graduate-level reasoning than GPT-4 and excels in tasks involving math, coding, and knowledge retrieval.
  • Claude 3 has been trained using publicly available and internal data, with hardware from Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud technology.

Title: Elon Musk’s xAI open-sources base model of Grok

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • xAI has released its Grok artificial intelligence (AI) model, providing details on its architecture and weights.
  • The Grok-1 version is a 314 billion parameter Mixture-of-Experts model trained from scratch by xAI.
  • The model was trained on a large volume of text data and is available on GitHub without the need for training code.
  • xAI's release of Grok is part of an effort to solicit input from the research community for potential enhancements.
  • Grok has been available to premium subscribers on X, the social media platform formerly known as Twitter.
