Title: Show HN: My related-posts finder script (with LLM and GPT4 enhancement)

Source: Tomhazledine.com

Summary:

  • The author used LLM embeddings to find related posts for their blog and GPT4 to explain why they're similar.
  • Embeddings are a way to make LLMs useful for natural language search without the problem of "hallucination" or incorrect content.
  • The author tried a manual approach and leaning on CMS features to generate related posts in the past but found limitations and issues with both methods.
  • Embeddings are vectors that represent the meaning of text and can be used to calculate the similarity between different pieces of content.
  • The author developed a node script that reads markdown files, generates embeddings, and uses GPT4 to generate explanations for the similarity of posts.

Title: Intel Neural-Chat-7B Model Achieves Top Ranking on LLM Leaderboard

Source: Intel.com

Summary:

  • The Intel neural-chat-7b model has achieved the #1 ranking for 7-billion-parameter models on the Hugging Face Open LLM Leaderboard.
  • The model achieved comparable accuracy scores to models 2-3x larger, making it suitable for deployment on a wide range of compute platforms.
  • The model is based on the Mistral-7B-v0.1 transformer model from Mistral AI, which achieved competitive benchmark results as a small LLM with a large context window.
  • The team used supervised fine-tuning and direct preference optimization (DPO) to attain the competitive benchmark score.
  • Intel Extension for Transformers and Intel Neural Compressor are available for fine-tuning, customizing, and optimizing the NeuralChat chatbot.
