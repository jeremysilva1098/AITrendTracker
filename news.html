Title: US FCC makes AI-generated robocalls illegal

Source: BBC News

Summary:

  • The US Federal Communications Commission (FCC) has made AI-generated robocalls illegal.
  • The move is aimed at addressing concerns around misinformation and manipulation ahead of the US election.
  • The use of AI-generated voices in robocalls has been linked to scams, fraud, and misinformation campaigns.
  • State attorneys general can now prosecute companies and individuals behind these calls for using AI-generated voices, expanding the legal avenues to hold perpetrators accountable.
  • The move follows a letter from attorneys general from 26 states urging the FCC to restrict the use of AI in marketing phone calls.

Title: AI cannot be used to deny health care coverage, feds clarify to insurers

Source: Ars Technica

Summary:

  • The Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) has clarified that health insurance companies cannot use algorithms or artificial intelligence (AI) to determine care or deny coverage to members on Medicare Advantage plans.
  • The clarification comes after lawsuits were filed against UnitedHealth and Humana for using a flawed AI-powered tool called nH Predict to deny care to elderly patients on Medicare Advantage plans.
  • The CMS memo states that coverage decisions must be based on the individual patient's circumstances, and algorithms that determine coverage based on a larger data set instead of the patient's medical history or physician's recommendations would not be compliant.
  • Insurers must reassess the individual patient's condition and provide a specific and detailed explanation if care is denied, including the applicable coverage criteria and rules.
  • The CMS also warns insurers to ensure that any AI tools or algorithms used do not perpetuate or exacerbate existing bias or introduce new biases.
