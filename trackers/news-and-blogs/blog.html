Title: Show HN: My related-posts finder script (with LLM and GPT4 enhancement)

Source: Tomhazledine.com

Summary:

  • The author used LLM embeddings and GPT4 to find related posts for their blog.
  • Embeddings are a way to make LLMs useful for natural language search without the worry of generating incorrect or "hallucinated" content.
  • The author tried manual approaches and leaning on CMS features to generate related posts in the past but found limitations and drawbacks with both methods.
  • Embeddings are mathematical representations of the meaning of text and can be used for calculating similarities between pieces of content.
  • The author developed a node script that reads markdown files, generates embeddings and summaries, compares similarities, and updates the related posts data in the frontmatter of the files.

Title: Intel Neural-Chat-7B Model Achieves Top Ranking on LLM Leaderboard

Source: Intel.com

Summary:

  • The Intel neural-chat-7b model has achieved the #1 ranking for 7-billion-parameter models on the Hugging Face Open LLM Leaderboard.
  • The model is at the low end of today's large language model sizes but achieved comparable accuracy scores to models 2-3x larger.
  • The model is based on the Mistral-7B-v0.1 transformer model from Mistral AI and was fine-tuned using Intel Extension for Transformers.
  • The fine-tuning process used direct preference optimization (DPO) which provides human preference feedback and is more computationally efficient than reinforcement learning.
  • The model can be deployed on a wide range of compute platforms and there are examples available for fine-tuning and customizing the chatbot.
