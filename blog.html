Title: Show HN: My related-posts finder script (with LLM and GPT4 enhancement)

Source: Tomhazledine.com

Summary:

  • The author used LLM embeddings to find related posts for their blog and GPT-4 to explain why they are similar.
  • Embeddings are a way to make LLMs useful without the worry of generating incorrect or "hallucinated" content.
  • The author tried manual approaches and leaning on CMS features to generate related posts in the past but found limitations and issues with both methods.
  • Embeddings are a mathematical representation of the meaning of a piece of text and can be used for natural language search and calculating relations between content.
  • The author developed a node script that reads markdown files, generates embeddings, compares similarities, and uses GPT-4 to generate explanations for why posts are similar.

Title: How close are we to peak AI?

Source: Business Insider

Summary:

  • OpenAI's GPT-4 remains unbeaten and no AI model has convincingly outperformed it since its release in March.
  • The question now is whether generative AI, the most popular form of AI, has already reached its peak performance.
  • While theoretical improvements are possible, achieving them in practice is challenging.
  • Google's Gemini Ultra, released as a response to GPT-4, only slightly surpasses GPT-4 on performance benchmarks.
  • The industry is still striving for artificial general intelligence (AGI), but there may be limitations with transformer models that hinder progress.
