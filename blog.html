Title: Anthropic says its latest AI bot can beat Gemini and ChatGPT

Source: The Verge

Summary:

  • Anthropic has announced the next generation of its AI models, Claude 3, which is now multimodal and claims to be more capable than ChatGPT and Google Gemini.
  • Claude 3 models can answer more questions, understand longer instructions, and provide more accurate results by understanding more context and processing more information.
  • The new models are less likely to refuse to answer prompts and can give near-instant results even when parsing dense material like research papers.
  • Opus, the largest and most intelligent model, outperformed most models in benchmarking tests, showing better reasoning and understanding of math and code.
  • Claude 3 models improve significantly over the previous Claude 2.1 model, with Sonnet being twice as fast and excelling in tasks requiring rapid responses.

Title: Here's everything you need to know about Gemini 1.5, Google's newly updated AI model that hopes to challenge OpenAI

Source: Business Insider

Summary:

  • Google is developing a new large language model called Gemini 1.5 to rival OpenAI's GPT-4.

  • Gemini 1.5 underwent enhanced safety testing to mitigate potential harm.

Title: Nvidia unveils AI chatbot ‘Chat with RTX’ for local PCs

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Nvidia has released an early version of its Chat with RTX, a demo that allows users to personalize a chatbot with their own content on Windows PCs.
  • The custom generative AI is free to download and requires an RTX 30 or 40-series GPU with at least 8GB of VRAM.
  • Chat with RTX can search YouTube URLs and transcripts for specific mentions and summarize entire videos.
  • Users can connect local files on their PC as a dataset to an open-source large language model, enabling queries for relevant answers.
  • The tool uses retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), NVIDIA TensorRT-LLM software, and NVIDIA RTX acceleration to bring generative AI capabilities to local, GeForce-powered Windows PCs.

Title: Anthropic claim new Claude 3 AI chatbot outperforms ChatGPT and Gemini

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Anthropic has launched Claude 3, a chatbot that claims to have reached "near-human" levels of performance and outperforms ChatGPT and Google Gemini in industry benchmarks.
  • Claude 3 offers three different models with varying levels of power and functionality: Haiku, Sonnet, and Opus.
  • Opus, the largest-capacity model, displays higher graduate-level reasoning than GPT-4 and performs better in tests involving math, coding, and knowledge.
  • Claude 3 has been trained using publicly available and internal data, and utilizes hardware from Amazon Web Services and Google Cloud technology.
  • Anthropic, founded by former OpenAI personnel, aims to disrupt the field of conversational AI with Claude 3.
