Title: Artificial intelligence vs machine learning: what’s the difference?

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Artificial intelligence (AI) is a collection of technologies that allows machines to think, learn, and solve complex problems.
  • Machine learning (ML) is a subset of AI that enables machines to learn and improve from experience without explicit programming.
  • AI represents the overarching principle of allowing machines to understand, reason, act, or adapt like humans, while machine learning focuses on building systems that can independently learn from and adapt to new data.
  • Machine learning algorithms are classified into four types: supervised, unsupervised, semi-supervised, and reinforcement learning.
  • AI and machine learning have various applications in the real world, including financial tasks, cybersecurity, and fraud detection in mobile apps.

Title: A former Google engineer was arrested for allegedly stealing AI secrets for Chinese rivals

Source: Engadget

Summary:

  • A former Google engineer has been arrested for stealing over 500 files containing AI trade secrets and using them to benefit rival tech companies in China.
