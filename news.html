Title: Google DeepMind's Demis Hassabis Says Gemini Is a New Breed of AI

Source: Wired

Summary:

  • Google has announced Gemini, a new AI model that can process information in the form of text, audio, images, and video, making it a "multimodal" AI.
  • Gemini is described as a "new breed" of AI that could lead to major breakthroughs in robotics and other projects.
  • The initial version of Gemini is available through Google's chatbot Bard, with a more powerful version, Gemini Ultra, set to be released next year.
  • Google is exploring how Gemini could be combined with robotics to physically interact with the world, and is also researching ways to improve the reasoning abilities of AI models.

Title: AI That Helps Us be More Human with Each Other

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Digital marketers have embraced AI for its efficiency in content creation, but this has resulted in a lack of human connection and homogeneity in content.
  • AI-powered translation apps, like those from Google, are breaking down language barriers and enabling communication between different linguistic backgrounds.
  • AI features in business tools like Salesforce and HubSpot help build complete customer profiles and assist in remembering important details.
  • Wearable AI devices, such as Humane's AI Pin, allow users to keep their phones in their pockets and not miss out on important events or information.
  • The potential of AI lies in its ability to augment human experiences, and there are positive signs that this is possible.

Title: Google launches its largest and ‘most capable’ AI model, Gemini

Source: ReadWrite

Summary:

  • Google has launched its latest AI model, Gemini, which is being touted as its largest and most capable model yet.
  • Gemini is the first model to outperform GPT-4 in various areas and is highly flexible, capable of running on data centers and mobile devices.
  • The model comes in three sizes: Gemini Ultra for highly complex needs, Gemini Pro for a wide scope, and Gemini Nano for on-device tasks.
  • Gemini is also the first model to surpass human experts in the Massive Multitask Language Understanding (MMLU) test.
  • Google plans to license Gemini through Google Cloud and use it in consumer-facing Google AI apps. However, it will not be available in Europe or the UK due to regulatory hurdles.
