Title: The Hollywood Strikes Stopped AI From Taking Your Job. But for How Long?

Source: Wired

Summary:

  • Hollywood unions, such as the Writers Guild of America and the Screen Actors Guild, resisted the threat of AI taking over their jobs through strikes and negotiations.
  • The unions sought protections against AI, such as preventing AI from being trained on their work or manipulating it without their permission.
  • The resistance against AI also extended to other industries, with authors and visual artists filing lawsuits against AI companies for using their work without permission.
  • Governments, including the US government, have started taking steps to curtail AI's impact on human work and provide support for workers facing labor disruptions from AI.
  • While AI has the potential to create new jobs in the field, there are concerns about job displacement, especially for entry-level positions, and the impact of AI on human bias and creativity.
